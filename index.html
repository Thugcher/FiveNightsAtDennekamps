Welcome to Dennekamp's

Meet the twisted figures that haunt the halls. Survive five nights if you can...

Edward Dennekamp

The proprietor of this decrepit place, Edward Dennekamp is the central, relentless threat. He moves with a calculated malevolence, making his presence known when least expected and draining your resources with cunning precision.

Neil Jones

An aggressive and swift animatronic, Neil Jones relentlessly pursues the night guard. His sudden appearances and unnerving speed demand constant vigilance and quick reactions to keep him at bay.

Blake Bailey

The patient lurker, Blake Bailey advances slowly but surely through the building. Her chilling, drawn-out sounds and tendency to remain hidden on camera feeds will test your nerves and resource management.
